Performance marketing for beginners — the complete 2025 roadmap

By ScaleBiz Academy · June 2025

Performance marketing is one of the most in-demand skills in India right now. Every D2C brand, every startup, every agency is hiring — and most candidates don't actually know how to run a campaign. That gap is your opportunity.

What is performance marketing?

Performance marketing is digital advertising where you only pay for measurable results — clicks, leads, sales, installs. It's the opposite of brand advertising where you pay for impressions and hope for the best.

The main channels are Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook), Google Ads (Search + Display + YouTube), and increasingly, influencer marketing tied to performance metrics.

The 90-day roadmap

Days 1–30: Foundations

  • Understand the marketing funnel — Awareness → Consideration → Conversion
  • Learn the core metrics: CPM, CPC, CTR, CPA, ROAS, LTV
  • Create a Meta Business Manager account and explore the Ads Manager interface
  • Run a ₹500 test campaign on any product — even your own

Days 31–60: Channels

  • Go deep on Meta Ads — campaign objectives, audience targeting, creative formats
  • Learn Google Search Ads — keyword match types, Quality Score, bidding strategies
  • Understand attribution — last click vs data-driven, UTM parameters, GA4 basics

Days 61–90: Optimisation

  • Learn to read a campaign dashboard and make decisions from data
  • Practice A/B testing — creative, copy, audience, landing pages
  • Build your first real case study — even if it's a small campaign
The biggest mistake beginners make is spending 90 days consuming content about marketing instead of actually running campaigns. Real learning only happens when you put money behind an ad.

How to get your first job or client
